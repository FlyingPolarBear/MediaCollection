allosterically activates the alpha catalytic subunit to
phosphorylate downstream effectors. Here, we report that
knockout of AMPKγ, but not AMPKα, suppressed phosphorylation

by energy starvation. We identified PPP6C as an

energy and to promote cell survival. Further

PPP6C regulated by energy stress in an AMPKγ-dependent
manner. Thus, AMPKγ senses cellular energy availability to
regulate not only AMPKα kinase, but also PPP6C phosphatase
